Investment Thesis

The Revenue Growth of Apple can be expected to be in the range of 2% to 3% over the next five years, with an Operating Margin of 31.3% in Year 5. The Discount Rate is set at 8%, and the EBITDA Multiple is estimated at 18x. Based on these assumptions, the intrinsic value of Apple Inc. (AAPL) is calculated to be $118 per share, indicating a potential overvaluation of approximately 41% compared to the current market price of $200 per share.
The Revenue Growth can be explained by the plateauing of the iPhone sales, which has been the main driver of Apple's revenue for the past decade. The company is now focusing on expanding its services segment, which includes Apple Music, Apple TV+, and Apple Arcade, to drive future growth. The Operating Margin is expected to remain stable due to the company's strong brand and pricing power, while the Discount Rate reflects the risks associated with investing in a mature technology company. The EBITDA Multiple is based on the average multiple of comparable companies in the industry.
