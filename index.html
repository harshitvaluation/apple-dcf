Apple DCF Valuation

A detailed discounted cash flow analysis of Apple Inc. (AAPL) to determine its intrinsic value.

Project Summary

This project provides a comprehensive DCF valuation for Apple, incorporating detailed financial projections and key assumptions. The analysis aims to estimate the fair value of Apple's stock based on its expected future cash flows.

Investment Thesis

Apple's strong brand loyalty, expanding services revenue, and potential for new product categories support a positive long-term outlook. However, the valuation is sensitive to changes in key assumptions, particularly the discount rate and revenue growth projections.
